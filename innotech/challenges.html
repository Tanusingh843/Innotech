Daily Stress Relief Exercise Challenge

Deep Breathing Exercise

Practice 4-7-8 breathing technique: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, exhale for 8 seconds. Repeat 4 times.

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Systematically tense and relax muscle groups from toes to head. Hold each tension for 5 seconds, then release.

Mindful Walking

Take a 10-minute walk, focusing on your breath and the sensation of each step. Notice your surroundings without judgment.

Yoga Stretch

Perform 5 minutes of gentle yoga stretches, focusing on neck, shoulders, and back to release tension.

Today's Stress Relief Challenge

Practice 3 different stress relief techniques today. Track your progress and how you feel!
